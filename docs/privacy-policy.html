Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-12

This privacy policy applies to the Yiwen MP Microsoft Edge extension ("Extension").

1. Data Collection

The Extension is a client-side tool that runs locally in your browser. It:

  • Does NOT collect personally identifiable information (such as name, email, phone, location, or IP).
  • Does NOT track your browsing history or web activity for analytics/ads.
  • Does NOT transmit page content, inputs, or usage data to any remote server owned by the developer.

2. Local Storage Only

The Extension may store user preferences/settings locally in your browser (via the browser's storage APIs). This data:

  • stays on your device,
  • is never sent anywhere else,
  • can be removed at any time by clearing site data / uninstalling the Extension.

3. Permissions & What They Are Used For

The Extension requests only the permissions necessary to provide its core functionality (for example):

  • storage: to save your preferences locally.
  • activeTab/tabs/host access: to operate on the current page when you actively invoke the Extension (for example by clicking the toolbar icon or using the context menu). Page content is processed locally, not exfiltrated.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs.

5. Your Choices

  • You can disable/remove the Extension at any time via Edge's extensions management page.
  • You can clear extension data via edge://settings/siteData (or by removing the Extension).

6. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date.

7. Contact
